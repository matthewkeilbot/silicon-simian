### Running logging requirements

- average heart rate
- RPE

### RPE scale (required)
Rate of Perceived Exertion — how hard the set/run felt:
- RPE 6 = easy, ~4 reps left in tank
- RPE 7 = moderate, ~3 reps left
- RPE 8 = hard but clean, ~2 reps left
- RPE 9 = very hard, ~1 rep left
- RPE 10 = max effort, no reps left

Default early-training bias: most work should live around RPE 6–8.
