# Pain and Symptom Log

Use this whenever pain, pinching, numbness, tightness, or a movement-related symptom shows up.
If required details are missing, ask follow-up questions before logging.

## Required fields
- Date
- Body area
- Symptom type
- Severity (1–10)
- Triggering movement/activity
- What changed after regression or stopping
